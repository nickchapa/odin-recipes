Egg Sandwich Recipe

Description

A simple egg sandwich with avocado

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • butter
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 slices bread

Steps

  1. Scramble or fry eggs.
  2. While eggs are cooking toast two slices of bread.
  3. Put eggs on one slice of toast.
  4. Spread avocado on the other slice of toast.
